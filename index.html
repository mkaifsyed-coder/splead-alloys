About Us

SPLEAD ALLOYS PRIVATE LIMITED is an environmentally conscious company specializing in recycling of SMF and Lead-Acid Batteries. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to sustainable practices, we transform hazardous waste into reusable materials that power industries while protecting our planet.

Our Services

Battery Collection & Recycling

Convenient pickup and responsible processing across India.

Lead Recovery & Refining

Smelting and refining lead for reuse in new batteries.

Plastic & Electrolyte Recycling

Plastic casings are repurposed, and electrolyte is converted into sodium sulfate for industrial use.
